Asteroids

Content about asteroids and their impact on space exploration.

Asteroids are remnants of the early solar system, composed mainly of rock and metal. Their study helps scientists understand the formation of planets and the evolution of our cosmic neighborhood.

By monitoring asteroid trajectories, we can assess potential threats to Earth and gain insights into the primordial materials that built our solar system.
